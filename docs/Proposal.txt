Group name: The Working Men

Canvas group number: 2

Project name: Major Potential

Proposal: To create a website that links universities, the cities where the universities are located, 
and job opportunities in cities for graduates in certain majors.

Model 1 - Universities
Size
Student-faculty ratio
Enrollment statistics / demographics
Media includes photos, location maps, university social media feed
Link to model 2 - Top majors are offered here
Link to model 3 - What city the university is located in, others nearby

Model 2 - Degree Plans, Majors
Jobs you could hold with this major
Starting salary statistics
Job growth rates
Media includes photos of job sites, etc
Link to model 1 - universities offering a curriculum in this major
Link to model 3 - cities with good job opportunities for this major

Model 3 - Towns, Cities
Population
State, county
Large companies operating nearby
Media includes photos, maps
Link to model 1 - universities located within or near the city
Link to model 2 - jobs/degrees which this city has good job opportunities
